## OpenLieroX Forum

The community discussion board is hosted on **GitHub Discussions** &ndash; a full
forum with topics, categories, search and replies. Sign in with any GitHub
account to read and post.

<div class="enhancement">

![Forum]({{ '/official/images/forum.png' | relative_url }}){:.enhancement_image}
[Open the OpenLieroX Forum &raquo;](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions){:.enhancement_link}<br />
Browse all topics, start a new thread, ask for help or share your mods, levels and skins.

</div>

Jump straight to a category:

- [General](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions/categories/general) &ndash; chat about anything OpenLieroX
- [Q&amp;A](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions/categories/q-a) &ndash; ask a question and get help
- [Ideas](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions/categories/ideas) &ndash; suggest features and improvements
- [Show and tell](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions/categories/show-and-tell) &ndash; share mods, levels, skins and screenshots
- [Announcements](https://github.com/openlierox/openlierox.github.io/discussions/categories/announcements) &ndash; news and release notes

You can also reach the wider community at the [LXAlliance Forum](http://www.lxalliance.net/forum/)
and on [SourceForge](https://sourceforge.net/projects/openlierox/).
